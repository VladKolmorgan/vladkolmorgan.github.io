What do I need to take care of my ants?

Ant care is important when you want to have your own colony! First you'll need a place to put them such as an ant farm along with some soil. Then you'll need ants, a queen to reproduce for the hive and some worker ants to do work such as tunnel building and obtaining food. Then you'll need to consider diet generally speaking things they would get in there own environment would be best. Ensure the proper amount of moisture and heat, and you're ant farm is ready to go! For a more detailed explanation try this post on wikihow.

Other facts about ants?

There are a lot of places to get some great ant facts! There are many online forums, games, educational books, and countless documents dedicated towards the sharing of Information about ants. We'll share some quick ones however such as there being over 12,000 different species of ants, ants can carry well over 10 times there own weight, and ant queens can live for several years before dying! Is there really 20 quadrillion ants?

No there really isn't. There is likely much more of them at this point, as the last time someone tried to accurately predict how many ants there were was in 2020. To make it even more complicated it was only an educated guess not an actual amount, so the number can be much higher or lower!
